Technical Proficiency

Our automated green sand and no-bake chemically bonded sand systems allow for rapid prototyping and high-volume production of components up to 15,000kg. We utilize advanced MAGMA soft simulations to predict solidification and minimize defects before the first pour.

Metallurgical Control

Full in-house chemical analysis via OES spectrometry ensures every heat meets exact customer specifications. We specialize in Grey Iron, Ductile Iron, and high-alloy Steels with certified grain structure analysis.

  • Extremely cost-effective for medium to large-scale parts.
  • Handles a vast range of materials, including high-melting-point alloys.

  • Capability to produce complex internal geometries with cores.

remove_circle Limitations (The Cons)

Future Capability

Investment Casting

A high-precision process using expandable wax patterns to create intricate, near-net-shape components. Ideal for aerospace turbines and medical implants where surface finish and dimensional tolerance are critical.

Planned Launch: Q4 2025
Knowledge Entry

High-Pressure Die Casting

Process involving forcing molten metal under high pressure into mold cavities. Primarily utilized for non-ferrous alloys like aluminum and zinc for high-volume automotive and consumer electronics parts.
