Cost-Efficiency &
Scalable ROI

Sand casting drastically reduces the initial capital barrier. By utilizing low-cost tooling and 100% reusable sand media, it is the most economical choice for both rapid prototyping and sustained production runs.

When to Choose Sand Casting

The Engineer's Decision Matrix

scale

Weight > 50kg

When the physical volume and weight of the part exceed the capabilities of high-pressure die casting machines.

timer

Rapid Prototyping

When the design is still evolving and you need functional test parts without committing to $100k+ steel dies.

science

Special Alloys

When high-temperature ferrous alloys like Stainless Steel or Ductile Iron are required for performance.

hub

Internal Complexity

When parts require internal water jackets, oil galleries, or hollow structural voids.
